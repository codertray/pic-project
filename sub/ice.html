Too Cool

My poor brother! we were elk hunting innorthern montana, and boy the day was turning out beautiful. we took a break and went walking to a small lake near the road. By small i mean real small. You could see the shore all around the lake. my brother was kiond enough to help medown the path because I nearly went tumbling down a hill I couldn't see. When we reached the lake it was covered with ice, so my brother starts taking ice from the shore, and spinning it onto the lake. Suddenly i was inspired! "can you grab a big sheet of ice, and hold it up for me to take a photo through?" I asked. This is the result. i love how itturned out. For those who may be interested I was using the Canon EOS m50 with the 15-45 kit lens.
